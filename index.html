Who We Are

About
JIN Properties

"Student housing should feel like home — not a compromise. We built JIN Properties to prove it."

JIN Properties is a Windsor-based business focused on providing luxury student rentals exclusively for students attending the University of Windsor. Every home in our portfolio is fully furnished and thoughtfully designed to give students a clean, comfortable environment where they can rest well, focus on their studies, and pursue a greater future.

From the materials we build with to the standards we hold every property to, every detail is shaped by one belief — where you live shapes how you learn.
